About us

OUR MISSION

As a responsible, customer-focused market leader, our purpose is to act for mankind progress by protecting what matters.

Protection has always been at the heart of our business, giving hands for those who in need, cherishing excellent relationships among our customers, and being a helping mechanism for country's economy. We, One future coporation, ensure that every customers are equally treated, and provided with immense level of support. As one of the largest insurers in Sri lanka, our mission is to create and lead humanity towards one bright future, enhances the beauty and values of life. Our purpose is shared by each and every employee throughout our organization, as we tailored our services and solutions for over 1 million customers throughout Sri lanka, in an ever-changing and fast evolving world.

OUR PROMISE

Create one bright future ahead. We promise to continue protecting life and forge life-long relationships with over 1 million citizens, and continue aiming at higher level until we have achieved our vision of protecting each and every Sri Lankan familly with our insurance solutions.
